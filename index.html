About IDR

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute preparing talent for digital, cyber and technology risk roles where security, compliance and operational resilience matter most.

The Institute of Digital Risk bridges the gap between talent development and employer demand in regulated, high-consequence sectors. Through focused learning pathways, applied projects and employer-aligned delivery, IDR helps build practitioners who are ready to contribute from day one.

Its model is strengthened by academic insight through a UK university partnership, paired with real-world projects, frameworks and deployment opportunities shaped by industry practice.

  • Industry-led curriculum shaped by live digital and AI risk priorities
  • Applied learning for regulated environments and mission-critical systems
  • Academic collaboration supporting rigorous, future-facing thinking
  • Practical deployment pathways for employers seeking ready talent

Who We Serve

IDR supports a growing community of learners, early-career professionals and practitioners building capability in digital and cyber risk.

Community focus

IDR serves students, career-switchers, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners seeking meaningful upskilling in digital, cyber, technology and AI risk disciplines.

The institute is especially relevant to sectors such as financial services, critical infrastructure, public services and other regulated industries where trust, security and resilience are central.

  • Students looking for a stronger entry point into digital risk careers
  • Professionals seeking structured upskilling in cyber and technology risk
  • Employers building talent pipelines for resilience and compliance functions
  • Practitioners navigating AI adoption, governance and emerging digital threats
